A new start

Hello everyone,

Recently I have been having a lot of trouble getting to work. It is just so easy to do anything else, even if it is worse than doing your job.

I should probably start this by introducing myself. My name is Damien (sometimes Damorin) and I am a research student at the Universtiy of Strathclyde for the Computer and Information Sciences department. My research is in the area of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and video games. The work I do looks at developing a method of creating AI which can solve unknown problems. I could go into this for quite a while, but that is not the point of today's post.

Today I want to focus a bit on my challenge in getting to work. How can I have an interesting and extremely fun project to work on, and yet procrastinate constantly? The answer is me. I am my own boss, and that does not appear to be wise. So I decided to create a new boss, as I am incompetent at it. That boss is you, the world. I want to create these blog posts to be accountable, to shout my intent to the world and hopefully feel guilty when I don't get enough work done.

Thank you for accepting this position of responsibility. Perhaps I can blame you going forward.

Each monday I will post on here with a plan of action. An overview of what I hope to achieve for the week. At the end of the week on Friday I will give an honest evaluation of how much I have achieved. I have put together a sort of work board in front of my desk to also constantly judge me.

This may seem a little sadistic, but my work is important to me. I would not feel right to not give it the fullest of my attention and so far it has been easy to read papers, facebook posts, go for a walk, look longingly at that cake. I need to focus, and I hope that this will help me to do so.

Thanks for listening, hopefully today is truly a fresh new start.

Damien/Damorin
